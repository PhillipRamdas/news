Our Goal

We set out to make a website to expose users to a wide variety of news sources. Modern news sources often target their userbase's interests, and their news feed becomes an echo chamber. For the sake of gaining the most complete understanding of news, as well as being exposed to new perspectives we hope this app serves you well!


For any concerns, recommendation, or feedback feel free to contact us using the form at the bottom of this page.
